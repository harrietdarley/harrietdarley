How to Play

Press "Play Easy" or "Play Hard" to begin the game. Answer the question by clicking on one of the options and press submit before the timer runs out. If the question is correct then the answer will turn green, however if it turns red it is incorrect. To pause or return home press the home button in the right top corner. To turn on or mute the music click on the volume button.

Game Modes

Easy Mode: The timer will be set to 1 minute.
Hard Mode: The timer will set to 30 seconds
